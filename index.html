About

The Craft Behind the Frame

I am a freelance writer and director based in Bangladesh, with a passion for visual storytelling that spans documentary, fiction, and commercial filmmaking.

My work lives at the intersection of authenticity and craft — whether scripting a brand story, directing a television commercial, or capturing the texture of real lives in documentary form.

I believe every project deserves a distinct voice. That conviction drives every frame I compose and every word I write.
